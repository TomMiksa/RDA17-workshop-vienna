NOVEMBER 23, 2017, VIENNA

RDA Europe Workshop
Data Stewardship Realized: From Planning to Action

Towards the Establishment of an Austrian Research Infrastructure

Research Data Alliance - RDA Europe will hold a one-day RDA Europe Workshop in Austria on 23rd November at the University of Vienna. The purpose of the event is to discuss the e-Infrastructures Commons and the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) and the development of national (“Use Case Austria”) and international research infrastructures.

The status on FAIR data, data policies, data management plans and KPIs will be discussed from various levels by a mix of domestic and European experts.

THE VENUE

The Campus of the University of Vienna has its historical roots in the 18th century, when Emperor Joseph II had what was then Vienna’s major poorhouse converted into the city’s first General Hospital. Ownership of the entire building complex was transferred from the City of Vienna to the University by way of gift in 1988.

The Campus of the University of Vienna is close to the historic city centre and easy to reach by public transport.
